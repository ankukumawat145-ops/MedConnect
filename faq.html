Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to your medical registry and transport queries.

Verification: How do we trust the doctors?
  • Manual verification of MCI/NMC registration numbers.
  • Direct background checks with AIIMS, Apollo, etc.
  • Verified badge for only document-verified professionals.
MedCab: Arrival time and tracking?
  • Average arrival time is 4-8 minutes in urban areas.
  • Live GPS tracking enabled for accurate location.
  • Direct communication link with the driver.
Payments: Any hidden clinical charges?
  • Zero hidden fees; transparency is our priority.
  • Consultation fee includes registration at the clinic.
  • Invoices generated instantly after booking.
Privacy: Is my data end-to-end encrypted?
  • Compliant with HIPAA and Indian Data Protection Acts.
  • Encrypted medical reports accessible only by your doctor.
  • Secure cloud storage for all patient records.
MedCab Prime: Oxygen and BLS Support?
  • Certified medical-grade oxygen cylinders available.
  • Trained paramedics for Basic Life Support during transit.
  • Standard medical kits in every MedCab vehicle.
Cancellation: How to get a refund?
  • Full refund if cancelled 12 hours before slot.
  • Instant refund to MedConnect Wallet on failed bookings.
  • 3-5 days for bank account transfers.
Tele-Health: How to start video call?
  • Access 'My Bookings' and click 'Join Call'.
  • Encrypted video link sent 10 mins before time.
  • Digital prescription available post-consultation.
Support: What if the doctor is not at clinic?
  • Report instantly through the 'Help' button.
  • Free rescheduling or full immediate refund.
  • 24/7 dedicated patient support line.
