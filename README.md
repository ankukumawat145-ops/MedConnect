MedConnect is a web-based healthcare platform designed to simplify access to medical services by integrating multiple healthcare features into a single digital system. The platform helps patients connect with healthcare providers and manage their medical needs efficiently through a convenient and user-friendly interface.
<br>
<br>
<br>
One of the main features of MedConnect is Online Doctor Consultation, which allows patients to consult doctors remotely for medical advice and follow-up care. The platform also includes MedCab, a transportation service that enables patients to book a cab to reach hospitals or healthcare centers quickly and reliably.
<br>
<br>
<br>
MedConnect also provides additional healthcare services such as Lab Test Booking and a Blood Bank module, helping users find nearby laboratories, schedule tests, and check blood availability during emergencies. To make healthcare more affordable, the platform includes a Generic Medicine Store where users can search for cost-effective generic alternatives to branded medicines.
<br>
<br>
The platform also features an AI Chat Virtual Assistant that helps users navigate the system and get quick responses to common healthcare queries. Additionally, MedConnect ensures secure medical record management with end-to-end encryption, protecting patient data while allowing authorized users to access medical history safely
